Our Menu

Tres Leches

Tres Leches is a moist and creamy cake soaked in a blend of three milks: evaporated, condensed, and heavy cream. Topped with whipped cream, it's a rich and delightful dessert that melts in your mouth.

Coconut Cake

Coconut Cake is a tropical treat made with layers of fluffy cake infused with coconut milk and topped with creamy coconut frosting. Each bite is a refreshing blend of sweetness and nutty coconut flavor.

Brownies

Our Brownies are a perfect balance of fudgy and chewy, rich with deep chocolate flavor. Whether enjoyed plain or with added nuts, they're a timeless treat that satisfies any chocolate lover's cravings.

Letocafe Cake

Letocafe Cake is a sophisticated dessert with layers of light sponge cake, smooth chocolate, and velvety cream. Finished with a glossy ganache, it's an elegant and indulgent treat for special occasions.

Vanilla Bliss

Vanilla Bliss is a classic, elegant cake that highlights the pure, fragrant flavor of vanilla. With its soft, moist layers and silky buttercream frosting, this cake is a timeless favorite.

Chocolate Indulgence

Chocolate Indulgence is a decadent cake for true chocolate lovers. Rich, moist, and packed with deep chocolate flavor, this cake is layered with smooth chocolate ganache for an irresistible treat.

Lemon Zest

Lemon Zest is a bright and refreshing cake that combines the tangy flavor of fresh lemons with a light, airy texture. Perfectly balanced with a sweet lemon glaze, it's a zesty delight.

Red Velvet

Red Velvet is a luxurious cake with a distinctive deep red hue and a hint of cocoa. Paired with creamy, tangy cream cheese frosting, it's a beautiful and indulgent dessert.

Caramel Crunch

Caramel Crunch is a cake that combines the richness of caramel with the satisfying crunch of toffee bits. The soft, buttery layers are filled with caramel sauce, making every bite a sweet indulgence.

Raspberry Twist

Raspberry Twist is a light and fruity cake that features layers of raspberry-infused cake and cream. The tangy sweetness of raspberries adds a fresh twist to this delightful dessert.

Mocha Delight

Mocha Delight is a rich and flavorful cake that blends the deep flavors of chocolate and coffee. The moist cake layers are paired with a smooth mocha frosting, making it a perfect treat for coffee lovers.

Coconut Tropic

Coconut Tropic is a tropical-inspired cake that combines the creamy sweetness of coconut with a light, fluffy texture. Finished with toasted coconut flakes, it's a taste of paradise.

Hazelnut Heaven

Hazelnut Heaven is a nutty and decadent cake that features the rich flavor of hazelnuts throughout. The layers are filled with a smooth hazelnut cream, creating a truly heavenly experience.

Berry Burst

Berry Burst is a vibrant and juicy cake bursting with the flavors of mixed berries. Each bite offers a refreshing blend of sweetness and tartness, perfect for berry lovers.

Almond Delight

Almond Delight is a nutty and elegant cake, featuring the subtle, sweet flavor of toasted almonds. With layers of almond-infused sponge and a creamy almond frosting, this cake offers a delicate, refined taste that's simply delightful.

About Us

Crafting Sweet Memories, One Cake at a Time



At Haya's Cakes, we believe that every cake tells a story. Founded out of a passion for baking and a love for bringing people together, our bakery has been crafting delicious, homemade cakes that make every occasion sweeter. We take pride in using only the finest ingredients, ensuring that each bite is a perfect blend of flavor and texture.

Our journey started in a small kitchen, where the aroma of freshly baked goods quickly became a beloved staple in our community. Today, we continue that tradition, creating cakes that are not only beautiful to look at but also a delight to taste. From classic flavors to innovative creations, every cake is made with love, care, and a commitment to quality.

Whether you're celebrating a birthday, wedding, or just the simple joys of life, our cakes are designed to make your moments memorable. We invite you to explore our range of cakes, each crafted to bring a little extra sweetness to your special day.

Thank you for choosing Haya's Cakes—where every cake is a masterpiece!
